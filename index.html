    About Christina

    Christina Early is the Area Councillor for Ward 2. She is running for the newly created position of Regional Councillor for Wards 1, 2, and 3. 

     

    Christina is a leader and community-builder. Following a 30-year career as a business executive, Christina ran for public office and was elected Area Councillor for Ward 2 in October 2018. 

     

    Christina is driven by a desire to contribute to creating a healthy, resilient, and inclusive Caledon. She sees the tremendous potential of a future Caledon that balances growth with its responsibility and commitment to environmental leadership. An active member of the community, Christina has served on numerous town committees and boards, including:

     

    • The Caledon Community Services Board ( Vice-Chair) 

    • The Caledon Public Library Board

    • The Toronto and Region Conservation Authority Regional Watershed Alliance

    • The Headwaters Tourism Board 

    • Accessibility, Audit and Procedural By-law committee

     

    Christina and her husband, Glen have two daughters, Jessica and Sara that they raised and are the fifth generation on the same family farm in Caledon.
